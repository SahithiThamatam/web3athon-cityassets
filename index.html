What is CityAssets

Chandigarh city has decided to make all the city assets like
Monuments, Lakes, Parks, Religious places, etc. on a public blockchain. This ensures that we give a bright future for our kids and make Chandigarh a carbon neutral city and saving natural resources.
Chandigarh city has provided a public facing website where all the information of the city assets can be authored, audited and published. AVAX blockchain is the partner chosen to publish all the assets as NFTs.
